Phases

My Process

1

Define The Problems

Identifying and clarifying the specific design issues and challenges faced by clients and stakeholders.

2

Define Background Research

Conducting thorough research on relevant industry trends, user needs, and technical requirements to inform design decisions.

3

Specify Requirements

Determining the functional and aesthetic requirements of the project, including materials, budget, timeline, and scope.

4

Brainstorm Solutions

Generating a range of creative and innovative design concepts to address identified problems and meet specified requirements.

5

Choose The Best Solutions

Evaluating the proposed solutions based on feasibility, usability, sustainability, and other criteria to select the best design direction.

6

Do Development Work

Translating the chosen design concept into detailed drawings, plans, and specifications, and overseeing the execution of the project from start to finish.
